# SICKLY DIGITAL PALETTESThis file documents the unwell color signatures of digital compression, sensor stress, and computational damage.## DefinitionSickly digital palettes refer to color distortions, imbalances, and artifacts specific to digital capture, processing, and compression—often with unpleasant or "poisoned" affect.## Digital damage color mechanisms### Chroma subsampling- Reduced color resolution- Color bleeding at edges- Blocky color transitions### Compression quantization- Banding in smooth gradients- Color shift in low-bitrate regions- Posterization### Sensor stress- Chroma noise (color speckle)- Magenta/green imbalance- Shadow color shift### White balance failure- Cyan/orange cast- Green/magenta split- Temperature confusion## Sickly palette categories### The Compression Cyan**Appearance:** Unnatural cyan dominance in shadows, orange push in mids**Causes:** H.264 chroma quantization, common error pattern**Affect:** Cheap, cold, clinical---### The Night Mode Magenta**Appearance:** Magenta chroma noise in shadows, greenish midtones, smeared detail**Causes:** Small sensor, aggressive noise reduction, high ISO**Affect:** Unnatural, surveillance, anxious---### The Banding Rainbow**Appearance:** Visible steps in gradients, color banding in skies, posterized skin**Causes:** 8-bit limitation, heavy compression, lifted blacks**Affect:** Cheap, broken, computational---### The Stream Buffer Shift**Appearance:** Color drifting over time, sudden quality drops, block color changes**Causes:** Adaptive bitrate, buffer issues, transcode variation**Affect:** Unstable, unreliable, network-anxious---### The Screenshot Generation Fade**Appearance:** Desaturated, contrast-flattened, color-shifted toward yellow or green**Causes:** Multiple recompressions, profile changes, platform processing**Affect:** Tired, circulated, meme-dead## Combining sickly palettesMultiple digital damages compound:- Compression + sensor noise = chroma speckle in banded regions- Multiple transcodes = shifting, unstable color- Screenshot decay + recompression = flat, wrong, tired## Prompting sickly digital> "H.264 compression cyan in shadows, magenta chroma noise crawling in darks, banded gradient steps in sky, aggressive noise reduction smear on skin, night-mode computational color"## SummarySickly digital palettes are the colors of computational struggle—chroma bleeding, quantization banding, and sensor stress creating unwell, anxious hues.
