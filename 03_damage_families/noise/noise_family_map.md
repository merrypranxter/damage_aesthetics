# NOISE FAMILY MAPThis file maps the complete noise family across all media contexts—distributed signal variation and particulate interference.## Core behaviorDistributed, semi-random signal or texture variation across the image field.## Family members### Film grain- **Medium:** photochemical film- **Character:** organic, particulate, stock-dependent- **Distribution:** tonally embedded, shadow-rich- **Motion:** subtle shimmer in projection### Sensor noise- **Medium:** digital camera sensors- **Character:** electronic, pixel-tethered, shadow-heavy- **Distribution:** ISO-dependent, chroma or luma- **Motion:** temporal crawl in video### Analog video snow- **Medium:** analog tape/broadcast- **Character:** signal-based, unstable field- **Distribution:** full frame, luma-dominated- **Motion:** dancing, field-structured### Compression chatter- **Medium:** digital compression- **Character:** quantization residue, edge-clustered- **Distribution:** block-adjacent, detail-dependent- **Motion:** shifts with keyframes### Scan noise- **Medium:** scanning systems- **Character:** electronic readout variation- **Distribution:** line-structured or random- **Motion:** static per scan### Toner speckle- **Medium:** laser/xerographic print- **Character:** powder distribution, electrostatic- **Distribution:** density-dependent, mechanical- **Motion:** N/A (static medium)## Cross-media stacks with:- low light / high gain- compression stress- age / degradation- signal weakness## Anti-flattening notesDo not treat all noise as identical "grain."Preserve:- medium origin- texture
