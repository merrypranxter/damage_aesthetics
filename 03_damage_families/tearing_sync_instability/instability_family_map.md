# INSTABILITY FAMILY MAPThis file maps the complete tearing/sync instability family across all media contexts—temporal and spatial frame disruption.## Core behaviorDisruption of stable image presentation through timing, synchronization, or mechanical failure.## Family members### Tracking error (tape)- **Medium:** analog videotape- **Character:** horizontal band displacement, rolling- **Cause:** head/tape misalignment- **Rhythm:** periodic, transport-tied### Sync loss (broadcast)- **Medium:** analog broadcast- **Character:** vertical roll, frame slip, tearing- **Cause:** signal timing failure- **Rhythm:** unstable, reception-dependent### Frame tearing (digital display)- **Medium:** digital display/graphics- **Character:** horizontal split, buffer mismatch- **Cause:** refresh/desync with frame buffer- **Rhythm:** motion-dependent, irregular### Gate weave (film)- **Medium:** film transport- **Character:** subtle frame jitter, vertical instability- **Cause:** mechanical transport variation- **Rhythm:** periodic, projector-specific### Rolling shutter (digital sensor)- **Medium:** CMOS sensors- **Character:** vertical shear during motion- **Cause:** sequential line readout- **Rhythm:** motion-speed dependent### Buffer stutter (digital playback)- **Medium:** digital video playback- **Character:** frame repeat, skip, freeze- **Cause:** data flow interruption- **Rhythm:** network/buffer dependent## Cross-media comparison| Aspect | Tape | Broadcast | Digital Display | Film | Sensor ||--------|------|-----------|-----------------|------|--------|| Direction | Horizontal | Vertical/roll | Horizontal | Vertical | Vertical shear || Cause | Mechanical | Signal | Buffer | Mechanical | Readout || Periodicity | Regular | Irregular | Irregular | Regular | Motion-based || Visual | Band noise | Roll/jump | Clean split | Subtle jitter | Shear |## Common stackingInstability stacks with:- mechanical wear- signal weakness- motion- buffer/network issues## Anti-flattening notesDo not use "glitch" or "jitter" as catchalls.Specify:- direction of disruption- mechanical vs electronic vs computational cause- periodic vs irregular behavior## SummaryThe instability family is the frame refusing to hold still—mechanical, electronic, and computational forces pulling the image out of stable presentation.
