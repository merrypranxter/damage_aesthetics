# SMEAR FAMILY MAPThis file maps the complete smear/drag/bleed family across all media contexts—directional spreading and leakage of image information.## Core behaviorDirectional redistribution of image content beyond original boundaries.## Family members### Chroma bleed (analog video)- **Medium:** composite analog video- **Direction:** primarily horizontal- **Cause:** bandwidth limitation, Y/C separation- **Character:** color leaking from saturated regions### Tape smear- **Medium:** magnetic tape- **Direction:** horizontal, transport-aligned- **Cause:** head contact, magnetic domain spread- **Character:** soft horizontal trails, luma/chroma separation### Optical grease smear- **Medium:** lens contamination- **Direction:** radial or irregular- **Cause:** diffusion through petroleum/organic residue- **Character:** highlight-dependent, creamy, irregular### Codec motion smear- **Medium:** digital video compression- **Direction:** motion-vector aligned- **Cause:** prediction error, temporal filtering- **Character:** block-structured, motion-following### Phosphor persistence blur- **Medium:** CRT display- **Direction:** motion-following- **Cause:** phosphor decay time- **Character:** brightness-weighted trail, exponential fade### Motion blur (camera)- **Medium:** any camera with slow shutter- **Direction:** motion-following- **Cause:** temporal integration during exposure- **Character:** continuous streak, optical## Cross-media comparison| Aspect | Analog Video | Optical | Digital | Display ||--------|--------------|---------|---------|---------|| Direction | Horizontal | Irregular | Motion-vector | Motion || Cause | Bandwidth | Diffusion | Prediction | Decay || Brightness dep | Low | High | Medium | High || Edge character | Soft | Creamy | Blocky | Fuzzy || Temporal | Static | Static | Dynamic | Dynamic |## Common stackingSmear stacks with:- motion- bandwidth limitations- contamination- display characteristics## Anti-flattening notesDo not collapse all "soft spreading" into generic blur.Preserve:- directionality- causation (optical vs signal vs computational)- brightness dependence- medium-native behavior## SummaryThe smear family is information refusing to stay bounded—color, light, and motion leaking, dragging, and spreading according to each medium's specific limitations.
