# HYBRID DAMAGE PROFILESThis file documents deliberate combinations of artifacts from multiple media systems—stylized stacks that create new aesthetic territories.## DefinitionHybrid damage profiles are intentional combinations of artifacts from different media, eras, or systems to create specific aesthetic effects beyond native medium behavior.## Chain Hybrid: Analog → Digital### Profile: "Digitized VHS Archive"**Stack:**- VHS base (softness, chroma bleed, tracking)- CRT display (phosphor bloom, scanlines)- Early digital capture (480i, slight blocking)- Platform recompression (banding, macroblocks)**Effect:** Authentic-feeling archive video with layered history**Use:** Documentary, found footage, archival aesthetic---## Cross-Medium Hybrid: Film + Digital### Profile: "Scanned Grindhouse"**Stack:**- 16mm film (grain, gate weave, scratches)- Dirty gate (hair, heavy dust)- Scanner artifacts (banding, slight moiré)- Digital cleanup artifacts (smear, noise reduction)**Effect:** Extreme archival distress with modern intervention visible**Use:** Horror, exploitation homage, degraded memory---## Temporal Hybrid: Past + Present### Profile: "Phone Filming CRT"**Stack:**- CRT display (raster, phosphor, bloom)- Phone sensor (rolling shutter, noise, sharpening)- Compression (macroblocks, temporal smear)- Platform upload (recompression, color shift)**Effect:** Contemporary documentation of obsolete technology**Use:** Nostalgia documentation, tech archaeology, liminal spaces---## Stylized Hybrid: Impossible Combinations### Profile: "Analog Ghost in Digital Machine"**Stack:**- Film grain and halation (photochemical)- VHS chroma bleed (analog video)- Datamosh residue (digital compression)- CRT phosphor trails (display)**Effect:** Deliberately anachronistic, temporally confused**Use:** Experimental, dream logic, haunted media---## Severity-Stacked Hybrid### Profile: "Collapse Stack"**Stack (bottom to top):**1. Film base (grain, scratches)2. VHS duplication (softness, chroma bleed)3. CRT display (bloom, scanlines)4. Phone capture (noise, rolling shutter)5. Platform compression (blocking, banding)6. Screenshot decay (extreme recompression)**Effect:** Maximum information loss, near-abstraction**Use:** Extreme degradation, signal death, archaeological depth---## Hybrid design principles### 1. Anchor mediumStart with one authentic base profile.### 2. Add chain logicEach layer should imply a transfer or capture event.### 3. Respect severity balanceDon't let one layer dominate unless intentional.### 4. Maintain legibility controlKnow when the stack becomes unreadable.### 5. Document the fictionHybrid stacks are deliberate constructions—own the artifice.## Common hybrid failures### Incoherent mixingVHS tracking + modern interframe melt with no chain logic### Severity overloadEvery artifact at maximum—visual mush### Anachronistic without intentClean digital + random film scratch overlay### Medium confusionArtifacts that imply contradictory capture chains## Prompting hybridsStructure prompts by chain:> "16mm film scanned to VHS in the 1980s, played on CRT television, filmed with phone camera, uploaded to social media"Each step adds appropriate artifact layer.## SummaryHybrid profiles are the creative misuse of medium authenticity—building impossible histories from real component parts.
