# RECOMPRESSION STACKSThis file documents the complex artifact layering that occurs when video or images pass through multiple platform transcoding pipelines—each adding its own compression signature.## DefinitionRecompression stacks are the accumulated codec, resolution, and format changes applied by platforms, apps, and services as media circulates through contemporary digital infrastructure.## Core visual identityThese stacks feel:- multiply processed- platform-branded in their damage- temporally layered- sometimes surprisingly specific- like the image has been through many hands## Common platform signatures### YouTube- multiple quality tiers- VP9 or H.264 artifacts- thumbnail generation- end-screen overlays### TikTok- aggressive vertical video compression- text and effect burn-in- multiple re-upload cycles common### Instagram- heavy H.264 compression- aspect ratio cropping- Stories vs Feed vs Reels different profiles### Twitter/X- aggressive image compression- video quality reduction- thumbnail extraction### Messaging apps- WhatsApp: heavy compression, resolution reduction- iMessage: quality preservation but format conversion- Telegram: variable quality settings## Accumulated artifactsEach recompression may add:- resolution reduction- codec artifacts (macroblocking, banding)- color space shifts- frame rate changes- audio sync issues- metadata stripping or alteration## Severity rangeS1–S2: single platform, mild compressionS3: multiple platforms, obvious artifact buildupS4–S5: heavy circulation, severe degradation, "platform-poisoned"## Confusion risks### Recompression vs original low qualityRecompression is layered journey; original is source limitation.### Platform-specific vs generic compressionEach platform has signature patterns worth distinguishing.## Stack partnersInherently stacked with:- original capture artifacts- deliberate edits and filters- platform UI elements- caption and text overlays## Prompting notesUseful:- multi-platform recompression stack- YouTube-to-TikTok transcode damage- viral video compression decay- platform-poisoned image quality## SummaryRecompression stacks are the passport stamps of digital circulation—each platform adding its own visa of compression, each journey leaving marks on the image's quality and character.
